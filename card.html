House Cat

House cats can sleep 12 - 20 hours a day.

  • Scientific Name: Felis Catus
  • Typical Weight: 7 - 15lbs
  • Normal Lifespan: 10 - 20 Years
  • Habitat: Your House

Ancient Egyptians may have first domesticated cats as early as 4,000 years ago. Domestic cats are one of the most popular pets in the world, there are over half a billion in the world.
